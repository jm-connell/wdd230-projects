Target Audience

The target audience of this site is local residents and business owners. People visiting the sites should be able to find information about local businesses, community events, and public resources. Anyone looking to participate and contribute to the community should be able to find valuable information and resources here.

Personas

Mike

Mike owns several restaurants of a popular fast food chain. He wants to get more involved in community events. He's looking to collaborate with other businesses that sponsor local events like barbeques and the yearly 5K race.

Gus

Gus is the owner of a family tire shop that has been doing business in Yakima for over 30 years. He wants to work with other businesses that work on cars so they can refer clients to each other in order to better serve customers according to their specialties and work experience. He's an experienced mechanic but doesn't know as much about things a body shop would work on.
